How should artwork be stored?
Paintings by Anna Seminskiy should only be stored in a dry space, strictly avoiding any direct sunlight. Do not clean the painting
Frames
Paintings of Anna Seminskiy are sold without frames, but for an additional fee, we do provide a frame for the artwork
