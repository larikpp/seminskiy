The process

Anna Seminskiy is a painter by calling. Through years, Anna developed her own painting technique using oil and acrylic on a premium cotton canvases.
All of Anna’s paintings are born during the nightlife in the city of London. While painting, she becomes unavailable for her friends and family
The canvas for most of the art pieces is made of premium cotton or linen. In addition, Anna uses acrylic and oil paint, usually mixed together.
Art is inspired by Anna’s life, Whether it’s memory from a childhood, a wonderful moment or an idea she want’s to convey. As a rule, the average time for a painting is 140 hours.

The process
Anna Seminskiy is a young artist based in London. Anna is a painter by calling. Her style is a mix of neo-impressionism and contemporary art.
All of Anna’s paintings are born during the nightlife in the city of London. While painting, she becomes unavailable for her friends and family
The canvas for most of the art pieces is made of premium cotton or linen. In addition, Anna uses acrylic and oil paint, usually mixed together.
Art is inspired by Anna’s life, Whether it’s memory from a childhood, a wonderful moment or an idea she want’s to convey. As a rule, the average time for a painting is 140 hours.
